## Character

With Joy, you can create and customize characters to maximize your productivity

## Character Creation 

Users can create unique characters in Joy, personalizing them with names and avatars. This feature allows for a more engaging and personalized AI interaction experience.

## Override AI Settings

Each character can have distinct AI settings. Users can override the default AI configurations at the character level, customizing aspects like AI service, model, and temperature. This flexibility means that different characters can be tailored for various needs, providing diverse AI interactions based on the specific requirements of each conversation.

## Character Templates

In the Character list view, there's Template button which leads you a library of powerful character templates where you can apply to yours.
