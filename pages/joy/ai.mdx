## AI

Joy AI is a user-friendly platform that connects you to a growing list of AI services. 

- [OpenAI](https://openai.com/): Provides access to advanced AI models like GPT (for natural language understanding and generation) and DALL-E (for image creation).
- [Anthropic Claude](https://claude.ai/): Offers AI services focused on understanding and generating human-like text, known for its nuanced and context-aware responses.
- [Open Router](https://openrouter.ai/): A platform that routes to various AI models, optimizing for the best response based on the query.
- [Perplexity](https://www.perplexity.ai/): Specializes in AI solutions that provide deep insights and complex problem-solving capabilities.
- [Google AI Gemini](https://deepmind.google/technologies/gemini/#introduction): Google's cutting-edge AI technology, known for its powerful search and analytical capabilities.
- [Mistral AI](https://mistral.ai/): Offers AI solutions tailored for specific industries and applications, delivering customized AI-driven results.

Whether it's language models, image generation, or other specialized AI functions, Joy AI provides a streamlined, user-friendly interface that caters to both enthusiasts and professionals in the field of AI. This integration allows users to explore and leverage the strengths of each AI service efficiently, making it a valuable tool for anyone looking to engage with the latest advancements in artificial intelligence.

## Use your own API Key

To fully utilize the capabilities of Joy, you'll need to obtain API keys for the respective AI services you wish to use. Since Joy is a UI-only platform, these keys are essential for accessing the underlying AI services provided by different companies, such as OpenAI, Google AI, and others

For more information how to unlock the ability to use your own API Key, check [Quick Start](http://docs.indiegoodies.com/joy/quick-start) guide
